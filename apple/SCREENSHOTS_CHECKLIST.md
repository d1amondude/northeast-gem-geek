# Gem Geek — App Store screenshots (ADHD slices)

## Sizes Apple wants (iPhone)
Primary: **6.7" / 6.5"** — Simulator **iPhone 16 Pro Max** or **iPhone 15 Pro Max** is fine  
(1242×2688 or similar — Xcode simulator export is OK)

Need about **3–6** screenshots. Minimum often **3**.

---

## What to capture (in order)

| # | Screen | How |
|---|--------|-----|
| 1 | **Home catalog** | Open app → Home with stone list + lab card |
| 2 | **Rare filter** | Turn on **Rare only** → show * stones |
| 3 | **Lab card + photo** | Pick Diamond/Ruby → specimen image visible |
| 4 | **Photo ID** | Photo tab (free trial OK) |
| 5 | **Paywall / Pro** | Tap Free/Pro badge → plans |
| 6 | **Calipers** (optional) | Measure section if easy |

Avoid: error screens, half-loaded network, personal data.

---

## How to take them (Simulator)

1. Xcode → run on **iPhone 16 Pro Max** (or largest sim)
2. Arrange the screen
3. Simulator menu: **File → Save Screen** (or **Device → Trigger Screenshot**)
4. Files land on **Desktop** usually as `Screen Shot ...`
5. Move into `~/Desktop/gemological-assistant/apple/screenshots/`

---

## Upload to Connect

1. Safari → App Store Connect → **Gem Geek**
2. **iOS App Version 1.0**
3. **Previews and Screenshots** → **iPhone 6.5/6.7"**
4. Drag screenshots in (order = store order)
5. **Save**
