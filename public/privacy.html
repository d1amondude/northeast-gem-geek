Privacy Policy

Northeast Gemological — Gem Geek
Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Northeast Gemological (“we,” “us”) collects and uses information when you use the Gem Geek mobile application and related web services (the “Service”).

1. Information we collect

  • Account / device identifiers — a device or account ID used to apply subscription status and free AI limits.
  • Usage data — which features you use (e.g. catalog browse, Photo ID, lab tools), AI request counts, and basic diagnostics (errors, app version).
  • Content you submit — text you enter (lab measurements, chat questions) and photos you upload for analysis. Photos are sent to our servers only to provide the feature you requested.
  • Purchases — subscription status processed by Apple (or Google on Android). We receive confirmation of purchase/restore, not your full card number.
  • Support — email and message content if you contact us.

2. How we use information

  • Provide the catalog, calipers, Photo ID, and Pro lab tools.
  • Enforce free-tier limits and Pro entitlements.
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with law and App Store / Play policies.

3. AI processing

Some features use third-party AI providers (e.g. Google Gemini) to analyze text or images you submit. Those providers process the content solely to return a result for your request. Do not upload content you are not allowed to share.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers that power hosting, AI, and analytics (under contract);
  • Apple / Google for in-app purchases;
  • Authorities when required by law.

5. Retention

We keep account/entitlement and usage records as long as needed to run the Service and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of account-associated data (see below).

6. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit (HTTPS) and limit access to systems that process your content.

7. Children

Gem Geek is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your choices

  • You can stop using the Service and delete the app.
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID / Google Play account settings.
  • Request access or deletion of account data by emailing support (below).

9. Account deletion

To request deletion of your Gem Geek account data, email support@northeastgemological.com with subject “Gem Geek account deletion” and the email/Apple ID associated with the account. We will delete or anonymize account-linked records within a reasonable period, except where we must retain records for legal or fraud reasons.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
